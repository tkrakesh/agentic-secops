# PB-008: DLP PII Leakage Mitigation

## Playbook Metadata
- **Playbook ID**: PB-008
- **Version**: 1.1
- **Severity**: LOW
- **SLA**: 4 hours
- **Approval Required**: Auto-approved.

## Action Sequence
1. **Log Scrubbing**: Trigger the 'Cleartext Scrub' tool for the specific log index.
2. **Rotate Secrets**: If the PII includes credentials, trigger secret rotation.
3. **Notify DevOps**: Open a low-priority ticket for the development team to fix the logging code.

## Containment Logic
High-confidence SSN/CC matches must be scrubbed within 1 hour of detection.
